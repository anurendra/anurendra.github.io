    • Publications

    • Anurendra Kumar, T.Guha and P.Ghosh "A Dynamic Latent Variable Model for Source Separation", Accepted in ICASSP'18: We propose a novel latent variable model for learning latent bases for time-varying non-negative data. We connect our proposed dynamic Dirichlet latent variable model (dynamic DLVM) model to the two popular latent basis learning methods - probabilistic latent component analysis (PLCA) and non-negative matrix factorization (NMF). We show that (i) PLCA is a special case of the dynamic DLVM, and (ii) dynamic DLVM can be interpreted as a dynamic version of NMF. The effectiveness of the proposed model is demonstrated through extensive experiments on speaker source separation, and speech-noise separation. [Link]
